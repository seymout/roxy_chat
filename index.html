Roxy AI Messaging Consent

Roxy AI is a conversational AI assistant that responds to user messages via SMS/MMS.

Users opt in by visiting this page and voluntarily initiating a conversation with Roxy AI or by texting START to the messaging number.

By starting a conversation, you consent to receive conversational SMS/MMS messages from Roxy AI.

Message frequency varies based on user interaction. Message and data rates may apply.

Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time.
Reply HELP for assistance.

This service may include responses containing text, links, phone numbers, and multimedia such as images or videos.
